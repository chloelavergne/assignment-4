Payment for admission to indoor pool, outdoor pool, sauna and whirlpool can be made by cash, cheque, Debit, Visa, Mastercard or American Express. The group rate is available on drop-in admissions only for groups of 10 or more of the same age group. Children under 9 must be with a responsible guardian. Note that our facilities are closed on statutory holidays. The outdoor pool is open from June 20 to September 15.
